Shakes on the Lake Grand Opening

by Miss Hall

Shakes on the Lake is now open. Have you ever been on the North Beach with a craving for a Rasberry (or any flavor) shake and didn't want to leave the beach and lose your parking spot? That is why the owners of Shakes on a Lake created their new store that will bring their delicious shakes right to you, you don't even have to leave the beach! Order in the app, text or call and you will be enjoying your time on the sand with a ice cold shake in no time.
