Key Demographics

Our core strategy focuses on two key demographics. The first families which we classify as parents and their children. We also want to attract extended families. We want to offer families unique experiences and food that will help them have fun while making memories together.

Our second demographis is local businesses, we do not want to highlight large national chains but instead want to highlight local business. We will highlight businesses such as food trucks, restaurants, farmers markets, musicians and other businesses that offer activities. Our goal it to bring families and these local businesses together.
