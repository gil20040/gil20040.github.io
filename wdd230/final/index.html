Provo Chamber Of Commerce

Our Mission & Vision

Our mission statement is to be the best business enablers by providing cutting edge techniques and training for entrepreneurs, helping connect you to a large network of local business owners, and connecting mentors and mentees so everyone can learn from each other.

Our vision is to become the #1 city to start a business in the world!

Provo Chamber of Commerce members are 3 times more likely to succeed with their small business opening. Existing business grow at a 20% higher rate than non Chamber members in the same industry. Be a part of a winning team that wants to help everyone succeed.
